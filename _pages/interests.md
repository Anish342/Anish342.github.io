## Interests

### Research Interests

I work at the intersection of machine learning theory and security, with a focus on making ML systems trustworthy and robust. My current interests include:

<ul>
<li> Federated Learning and privacy-preserving ML </li>
<li> Adversarial robustness and model security </li>
<li> Intellectual property protection of neural networks (watermarking, fingerprinting) </li>
<li> Uncertainty quantification and probabilistic modeling </li>
</ul>

### Other Interests

Outside of research, I enjoy following financial markets and quantitative methods — particularly time-series modeling, signal extraction, and market microstructure. I also enjoy cricket, chess, and exploring new places.
